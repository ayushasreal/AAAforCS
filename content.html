Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR)

VR lets you feel like you're inside a game world using a headset. AR adds game elements to the real world, like Pokemon GO. In the future, VR could be cheap and used for school lessons or training. Example: Games like Beat Saber help with hand-eye coordination. This relates to Class 10 IT topics on emerging tech.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Gaming

AI is like a smart computer brain. In games, it makes characters act real and changes the story based on what you do. Soon, AI will make games personal for each player. Chart example: AI in games is growing fast in India (placeholder for a simple chart).

Esports and the Metaverse

Esports are competitive games, like tournaments in India. The metaverse is a big virtual world where you can play and meet friends. Platforms like Roblox are examples. In the future, esports could be like Olympics, and metaverse for learning. Example: Fortnite events in India bring people together.

Problems and Chances

Problems: Too much gaming can affect health or studies. But chances: Games can teach math, history, or teamwork. In India, cloud gaming (playing without a strong computer) will make it easier. Think about careers in game design!

  • Bad: Online safety risks, like cyber threats.
