About E-Gaming

E-gaming includes video games, online competitions (esports), and fun digital activities. It started in the 1970s with simple games like Pong and now it's a big industry in India and the world. In Class 10 Computer Science, you learn about tech like AI and VR, which are making games better. The future of e-gaming will use these to teach, heal, and connect people. This site helps you understand trends, problems, and chances in gaming.

  • History: Games began with arcade machines and grew to mobile apps like PUBG.
  • Now: Esports events in India attract lakhs of viewers, like cricket matches.
  • Future: AI will make games smarter, and metaverse (virtual worlds) will mix gaming with real life.
