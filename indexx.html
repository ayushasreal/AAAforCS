Welcome to the Future of E-Gaming

This website is for Class 10 students to learn about e-gaming. E-gaming means electronic gaming like video games and esports. We talk about new technologies like VR (Virtual Reality, where you wear glasses to feel inside a game) and AI (Artificial Intelligence, smart computers that learn). Our goal is to show how gaming is changing and can help in school and jobs. If you like games or want to know about tech, read on!
